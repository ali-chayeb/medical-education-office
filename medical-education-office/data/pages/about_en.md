  ## Our Mission
  
  The Medical Education Office at Aleppo University Hospital was established to develop and organize continuing medical education programs and clinical training for healthcare professionals.
  
  ## Our Vision
  
  We strive to be a leading center in medical education at the national level, keeping pace with the latest international standards in physician training.
  
  ## Our Responsibilities
  
  - Organizing training courses and workshops
  - Supervising medical residency programs
  - Evaluating trainee performance
  - Collaborating with local and international medical institutions
